Application Overview

Start to Finish is a running app that helps people who want to enahnce thier running perfornaces. It records, pace, distance, and time to keep runners on pace. It also gives feedback during your runs, like when you slow down or speed up. It helps runners avoid overtraining or undertraining by suggesting rest days or harder workouts and showing their progress over time.
